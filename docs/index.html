In many cases, yes. Common issues like virus removal, software troubleshooting and minor hardware fixes can often be completed same-day. Larger repairs such as screen or motherboard replacements may take longer depending on parts availability. Get in touch with your problem and Dave will give you an honest turnaround estimate.

Dave Fixes Tech covers Brisbane CBD and surrounds, Ipswich and surrounds, and suburbs within approximately 60km including the Redlands, Logan, Moreton Bay and Springfield areas. See the full list of suburbs in the Service Areas section above, or get in touch to check your location.

Can you set up and troubleshoot Starlink internet?

Yes. Dave Fixes Tech offers full Starlink installation, dish positioning, cabling and router setup, plus ongoing troubleshooting if your connection is dropping out or underperforming.

Yes, alongside laptop, PC and device repairs, Dave Fixes Tech also repairs gaming consoles including common issues like overheating, disc drive faults and controller problems.
